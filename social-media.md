# Social Media Posting Schedule

Building in public requires consistent content. Follow this schedule.

---

## LinkedIn (2-3 posts/day)

| Time | Content Type |
|------|-------------|
| 8-9 AM | Technical deep-dive or tutorial |
| 12-1 PM | Progress update or milestone |
| 5-6 PM | Reflection, lesson learned, or teaser |

---

## Twitter/X (4-6 posts/day)

| Time | Content Type |
|------|-------------|
| 7-8 AM | Good morning + what you're working on |
| 10-11 AM | Technical insight or tip |
| 12-1 PM | Progress update or screenshot |
| 3-4 PM | Question or engagement post |
| 6-7 PM | Day summary or milestone |
| 9-10 PM | Thread or longer-form content |

---

## Discord/Communities (2-3 posts/day)

| Time | Content Type |
|------|-------------|
| Morning | Daily goal/focus |
| As completed | Quick wins and updates |
| Evening | Summary and next steps |

---

## After Every Work Session

1. Update Daily Social Posts document with ready-to-post content
2. Create posts for EACH time slot based on work completed
3. Include:
   - Technical details
   - Lessons learned
   - Metrics and progress
4. Mark completed posts in the checklist

---

## Content Guidelines

- Be specific about technical work
- Share real numbers and metrics
- Include screenshots when possible
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Show the human side of building
